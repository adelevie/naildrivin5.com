--- wordpress_id: 20 title: Test Your Reflex ROCK wordpress_url: http://www.naildrivin5.com/daveblog5000/?p=20 layout: post --- A while back, I went to see Electric Six at the Black Cat. The opening band was so-Cal's Test Your Reflect, who play poppy 80s-inspired new-new-wave, along the lines of The Bravery. They absolutely rocked and jammed nonstop for their 40 minute set. Electric Six looked so lame in comparison (not to mention that Electric Six's songs are fairly weak). I went to their merch table to pick up their CD and was given a sampler with a couple songs on it; their CD wasn't out til later in the month. Really annoying; how could they put on such a manly rock show and then not have their CD?!?!? I went home and ripped the tracks off the sampler, and then Audio Hijacked the tunes off their myspace page. I forgot when their album came out until yesterday, when I picked it up from iTunes (get it here). Needless to say, it captures their live show and is my new favorite album. Start-to-finish, each song is a hit, save for the slow one (there's always a slow number, and they always suck). The single, "Pieces of the Sun", is a great song, but not the stand out track. For me, the bittersweet "Forever Young" updated "I'm Not Sorry" is one of the best 5 minute songs ever, but "New Year" is just a non-stop upbeat rocker. I was singing along with the chorus halfway through the first one. Anyway, this shit is on. I also picked up !!!'s latest, which is pretty lame. A few nice grooves, but mostly just meandering pointless jamming.
